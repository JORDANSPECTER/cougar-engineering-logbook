# Purpose

This roadmap defines the long-term engineering journey from software developer to AI Infrastructure Engineer while building Project Cougar.

Unlike a traditional learning roadmap, this plan is directly tied to real-world engineering work. Every skill is learned by designing, implementing, documenting, and operating components within the Cougar Platform.

---

# Vision

Build the engineering expertise required to design, deploy, and operate enterprise-scale AI platforms capable of supporting autonomous organizations, intelligent workflows, and distributed AI systems.

---

# Long-Term Objective

Become an engineer capable of architecting and operating systems comparable in complexity to:

- OpenAI Platform
- Anthropic Infrastructure
- Microsoft Copilot
- AWS AI Services
- Google Vertex AI
- Enterprise AI Operating Systems

The goal is to combine deep infrastructure knowledge with practical experience gained through Project Cougar.
