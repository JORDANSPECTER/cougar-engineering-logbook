# Architecture Principles

---

# Purpose

These principles guide every architectural decision made within Cougar OS.

Whenever multiple solutions are possible, these principles should be used to evaluate the best long-term approach.

---

# Principles

## Enterprise First

Every feature should be designed as part of a larger enterprise platform rather than as an isolated application.

---

## Modular Design

Systems should be divided into reusable, independent components with clear responsibilities.

---

## Single Responsibility

Every component should have one clear purpose.

---

## Reusable Before Duplicate

Shared functionality should become reusable services rather than duplicated logic.

---

## Security by Default

Security is designed into the platform rather than added after development.

---

## Knowledge Preservation

Engineering knowledge should be preserved through documentation and organizational memory.

---

## Scalability

Architecture decisions should support long-term growth rather than short-term convenience.

---

## Explainability

Complex systems should remain understandable by future engineers.

---

## Organizational Design

Every capability should belong to an organizational home.

Examples include:

- Foundry
- Executive Lane
- Platform Service
- Mission Node
- Private Lane

---

## Continuous Improvement

Architecture should evolve through deliberate improvements rather than large rewrites.
