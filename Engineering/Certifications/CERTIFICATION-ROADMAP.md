# Purpose

This roadmap defines the professional certifications that support long-term growth as an AI Infrastructure Engineer.

Certifications are used to validate practical skills developed through Project Cougar. They complement real engineering experience rather than replace it.

---

# Certification Philosophy

Project Cougar is the primary engineering laboratory.

Certifications serve to:

- Validate knowledge
- Fill technical gaps
- Demonstrate professional competency
- Strengthen career opportunities

The priority is always:

1. Build
2. Document
3. Operate
4. Validate through certification
