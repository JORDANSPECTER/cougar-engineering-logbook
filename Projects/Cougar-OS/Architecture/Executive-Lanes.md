# Purpose

Executive Lanes™ are the executive leadership layer of Cougar OS.

Each Executive Lane represents a permanent organizational executive responsible for a specific business domain.

Rather than using isolated AI assistants, Cougar organizes intelligence into an executive leadership team that continuously collaborates to operate an organization.

---

# Vision

Every organization deserves an executive team that:

- Never forgets
- Continuously learns
- Explains its decisions
- Coordinates across departments
- Operates with governance
- Assists humans rather than replacing them

Executive Lanes transform AI from individual assistants into an executive operating model.

# Executive Council™

The Executive Council™ coordinates the Executive Lanes.

Responsibilities include:

- Strategic planning
- Executive recommendations
- Organizational priorities
- Cross-lane collaboration
- Mission approval
- Organizational governance

The Founder remains the final decision maker.

---

# Private Lane™

# Relationship with AI Workforce

Executive Lanes lead departments.

Departments supervise specialist agents.

Specialist agents create temporary Mission Nodes.

```
Engineering Lane
        │
Engineering Department
        │
Specialist Agents
        │
Mission Nodes
```

This creates a scalable organizational hierarchy.

---

# Governance Principles

Executive Lanes follow these permanent principles:

1. Every Lane owns a defined domain.
2. Lanes collaborate rather than compete.
3. Decisions are explainable.
4. Executive Council coordinates cross-lane work.
5. Founder maintains final authority.
6. Organizational memory supports every decision.
