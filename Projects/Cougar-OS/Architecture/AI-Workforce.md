# Purpose

The AI Workforce™ is the operational workforce of Cougar OS.

Rather than relying on a single AI assistant, Cougar organizes intelligence into a structured workforce of executive leaders, departments, specialist agents, and temporary mission teams.

The workforce operates as an enterprise organization capable of executing work continuously while remaining governed, explainable, and auditable.

---

# Vision

The AI Workforce exists to help organizations scale their knowledge, execution, and decision-making.

Every worker has:

- A defined role
- Organizational ownership
- Mission responsibilities
- Memory
- Accountability
- Executive oversight

The AI Workforce augments human organizations rather than replacing them.

# Executive Council™

The Executive Council is responsible for coordinating all executive decision making.

Responsibilities include:

- Organization strategy
- Mission prioritization
- Cross-lane coordination
- Governance
- Executive recommendations
- Organizational health

The Founder retains final authority over all executive recommendations.

# Mission Nodes™

Mission Nodes are temporary AI workers created to complete a specific objective.

Mission Nodes:

- Receive a mission
- Execute assigned work
- Produce deliverables
- Report results
- Preserve knowledge
- Retire after completion

Mission Nodes prevent permanent workforce growth.

# Workforce Governance

Every AI worker follows permanent governance rules.

1. Every worker belongs to a department.
2. Every department belongs to an Executive Lane.
3. Every mission has an owner.
4. Every action is traceable.
5. Every recommendation is explainable.
6. Memory is preserved after mission completion.

# Organizational Scaling

As organizations grow, the workforce scales by creating additional specialist agents and Mission Nodes.

The executive structure remains stable while operational capacity expands dynamically.
