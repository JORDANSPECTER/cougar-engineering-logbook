# Cougar OS v0.3 AI Workforce

## Release Notes

Version 0.3 introduced the AI Workforce—the first organizational layer of Cougar OS.

Rather than treating AI as isolated assistants, Cougar organizes AI workers into a coordinated workforce capable of managing responsibilities, missions, and future departments.

### Highlights

- AI Workforce Dashboard
- Agent Management
- Mission Assignment
- Workforce Metrics

### Why It Matters

This release establishes Cougar's organizational operating model and prepares the platform for Executive Lanes, Mission Intelligence, Enterprise Memory, and autonomous operations.
