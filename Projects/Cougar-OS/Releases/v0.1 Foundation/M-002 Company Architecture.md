# M-002 Company Architecture

**Release:** v0.1 Foundation

**Status:** ✅ Complete

---

# Objective

Design Cougar as an organization rather than a single application.

---

# Engineering Work

Established the foundational organizational architecture including:

- Cougar Technologies
- Cougar OS
- Cougar HQ
- Cougar Trade

Defined how each system contributes to the broader platform.

---

# Architecture Decisions

Key principles introduced:

- Every major capability belongs to an organizational home.
- Shared platform services should support multiple products.
- Architecture should scale through modular systems rather than monolithic applications.

---

# Outcome

A clear separation of responsibilities was established between products, platform services, and future expansion.
