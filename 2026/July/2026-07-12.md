# Engineering Decisions

Engineering documentation will no longer interrupt active development.

Documentation becomes part of the engineering closeout process.

This preserves development momentum while maintaining a complete engineering history.

---

# Lessons Learned

Engineering systems become significantly easier to maintain when documentation is generated after implementation rather than during implementation.

The Engineering Operating System should support engineering—not compete with it.

---

# Challenges

Several organizational approaches were evaluated before selecting a timeline-first architecture.
